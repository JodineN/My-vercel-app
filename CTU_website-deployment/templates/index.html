About Us

CTU Training Solutions is a private higher education institution established in 1987. The institution provides career-focused education and training through its Career and Corporate divisions, helping students and professionals develop skills that meet industry demands.

Our Commitment

At CTU, we are committed to fostering innovation, creative thinking, and technological proficiency. Through a variety of study options across faculties such as Information Technology, Design, Business, Engineering, and Humanities, we prepare students for successful and rewarding careers.
